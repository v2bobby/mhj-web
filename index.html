THE REALITY IN LAGOS

A food system
under pressure.

Protein Deficit

Lagos consumes 11 million eggs daily with a structural 40% local supply deficit, causing price volatility and inconsistent quality.

Soil Degradation

Decades of synthetic NPK overuse have stripped urban soils of microbial life, reducing water retention and long-term food resilience.

Waste as Liability

Traditional poultry operations generate nitrogen-rich waste that contaminates waterways and destroys potential economic value.

THE MAYHAJ MODEL

A closed-loop ecosystem.
Zero waste. Maximum value.

01 — PRECISION POULTRY

200 Isa Brown Layers

Biosecure container housing engineered for Lagos climate. Consistent 85–90% lay rates year-round with superior disease protection.

02 — THERMOPHILIC COMPOSTING

28-Day Bio-Chemistry

Three-phase process: Heating (60°C pathogen kill), Curing (oxygenation), Refining (premium texture for urban gardeners).

03 — DUAL REVENUE

Eggs + Premium Fertilizer

Grade-A brown eggs for bakeries & households. Odor-free, refined organic fertilizer for urban gardens and estates.
